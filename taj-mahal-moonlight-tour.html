Taj Mahal Moonlight Tour from Delhi

As the orange light of Sun gets diminished and the whole sky turns into blue on full moon night Taj Mahal starts glittering in light blue color which is the most appealing and charismatic view of this amazing monument. Taj Mahal Moonlight tour provides all the necessary things which need while visiting on that night. The white marble of this monument has some magical things which change color as the day and night come.

Taj Mahal Private Tour brings up with Taj Mahal Moonlight Tour , in this tour tourists get an opportunity to explore the mesmerizing beauty of Taj on the occasion of full moon night. As it is a special tour of Taj Mahal in the mid-night, you need to check the availability of the tour. People can have this beautiful chance to explore this amazing moment only 5 days of a month.

Tour Plan

01

Pick up from Delhi :

In the Morning of the date when the tour starts our representative will pick up you from New Delhi either the location is your hotel or the airport. Have a ride in an AC car which is heading straightly towards Agra, and the distance can be covered in 3 hours by Yamuna Expressway. Upon arrival in Agra, check into your hotel. Enjoy a delicious lunch and have some rest for a while.

02

Agra Fort :

In this tour, our first visit is Agra Fort in the evening. It is a massive fort built by Mughal Emperor, Akbar. Agra Fort is a historical monument which is also famous for its beautiful architecture. The Fort was built by Mughal Emperor Akbar as a residence of the royal family and capital of his empire.

Located at a distance of 2.5km from Taj Mahal, Agra Fort is built in red sandstone. The craftsmanship and superb architecture of this monument invite millions of tourists every single year. Also, the fort serves as a museum housing the weapons, dresses and other belongings of the erstwhile rulers that are a big attraction for the tourists.

After visiting Agra Fort, return to your hotel. Enjoy a delicious dinner in your hotel before the next adventure.

03

Taj Mahal:

As the moon is ready to spread its shine on the earth, reach Taj Mahal to see the cool moonlights falling on the crystal white marble. In the moonlight, the monument changes its color every minute, and each transforming color is just magical. The entire atmosphere is hypnotic with couples spending their time with their loved ones, appreciating the beauty of the monument. While a day tour to Taj remains a hustle of exploring every corner of the monument, the moonlight view doesn't require anything else other than standing in front of the monument and watching the white color transforming in hues.

This is also the time when the cool breeze becomes more soothing. The calm and composed environment is sufficient to bring the other person out of you, allowing you to forget your worries and focus on what's in front of your eyes.

After exploring this majestic sight, return to your hotel for overnight stay. The journey resumes on next day.

04

Day 2: Fatehpur Sikri and Departure:

Though it’s a dedicated moonlight tour, we won’t let go some moments of Sunrise view of Taj. Leave the hotel in the morning and reach Taj Mahal to enjoy the sunrise view. This monument shows the different color of it as a comparison to in the past night. Enjoy the moment and with a smile just say goodbye to Taj.

Next destination is FatehpurSikri, a historical town located 45km east from Agra. The town was established during the Mughal Dynasty as the capital of the Empire. However, the rulers had to move away after a severe drought hit the town. You can still witness the proud monuments speaking the tales of the powerful empire.

Continue driving towards Delhi and conclude your journey there. We shall drive you back to your hotel or Indira Gandhi International Airport. The Taj Mahal Moonlight Tour ends here. Thanks for being a wonderful traveler.

*Note - The below itinerary is a suggested one. We can do customization in Taj Mahal Moonlight Tour package to make an itinerary as per your comfort, special interest and requirement which can be adding new places to visit or changing the sequence of whole package.

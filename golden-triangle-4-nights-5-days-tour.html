Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days

Are you willing to explore the colors of India? Why not take 5 days and 4 nights Golden Triangle tour which gives lifetime opportunity to explore India's distinct colors, the culture, and tradition. To take a dip into India's rich tradition, rich culture and history, do consider Delhi, Agra and Jaipur tour.

These three cities are prominent from the point of India's history. Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days will give you the most memorable moments for you will visit the major attractions of these three cities. Visit the Jama Masjid, take a rickshaw ride in Chandni Chowk and experience the mesmerizing beauty and charm of the Taj Mahal in Agra. The multi-day tour allows you to experience the best of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur.


4 Nights 5 Days Golden Triangle Tour Highlights

  • Visit the popular Lotus Temple, Red Fort, and Qutub Minar.
  • Shop at Chandni Chowk and enjoy the rickshaw ride in the bazaar.
  • Visit the Taj Mahal at Agra, Agra Fort, and Jama Masjid.
  • Explore Hawa Mahal, Jaigarh Fort and Amber Fort in the Pink City Jaipur.
  • A visit to Fatehpur Sikri in between.

Tour Plan

01

Day 1:

Arrive in Delhi and explore the major attractions. Our representative greets you at the airport and drops you to the hotel. We will start the tour from Old Delhi and explore Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb, Raj Ghat, and Lotus Temple. Later on, you may tour India Gate, President House, Parliament House, and other places. Stay in the hotel for the night.

02

Day 2

Early in the morning after completing breakfast, we drive to Agra which is 210km away from Delhi. From the Yamuna Express Highway, we drive for 3 hours and reach Agra. Just after lunch, you proceed for the guided tour and move to Itmad Ud Daulah or the Baby Taj. Then we visit Mehtab Bagh for that amazing sunset view of the Taj Mahal. Stay overnight in Agra.

03

Day 3

In the morning, you leave for the guided tour and visit The Taj Mahal to enjoy the sunrise view. After seeing the sunrise on the Taj Mahal, return back to the hotel. Have your breakfast and then leave for Agra Fort.

Have lunch in a multi-cuisine restaurant and explore the local arts and crafts. We leave for Jaipur from Fatehpur Sikri and reach out there by 7-8 PM. Stay overnight in Jaipur.

04

Day 4

The day-04 is spent on Jaipur sightseeing. After breakfast, the breathtaking Jaipur sightseeing tour begins. Visit the Amber Fort and enjoy the elephant ride. After this, visit the City Palace, Jantar Mantar or Observatory. Click pictures at Hawa Mahal and Jal Mahal. At last, we visit Birla Temple and in the evening shop for textile and Jewellery. Stay overnight in Jaipur.

05

Day 5

On the last day of the tour, you are driven back to Delhi from Jaipur by road. It will take nearly 5 hours by road.

Delhi, Jaipur, Agra, the three North Indian cities, represent old culture and tradition of India. So, explore the heritage sites and create unforgettable memories.

Tour Location

Explore Agra's Taj Mahal, Delhi's Red Fort and Jaipur's Amber Fort in 4 nights. A whirlwind journey through India's Golden Triangle, showcasing history, architecture, and culture.
